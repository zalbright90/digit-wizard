Getting Started:

Display Area: The top display shows your current calculations and results in a mystical red glow.

Basic Operations:

  • Use +, -, *, / for addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • C clears the current calculation.
  • ← erases the last digit.
  • % converts numbers to magic percentages.
  • +/- toggles between positive and negative energy.

Performing Calculations:

  • Simply click on the buttons to conjure numbers and operations.
  • Use the = key to unleash the magic and see your result.

Advanced Features:

  • Feel the power of . for decimal precision.

Keyboard Sorcery:

All spells can be cast using your keyboard's numpad for swift calculations.

Enjoy your journey with Digit Wizard - where calculations meet wizardry!

Pro Tip:

Explore the enchanting hover effects on buttons to sense their arcane power.
